About Us

> INITIATIVES

Angat Buhay: Advocating for Quality Education in the Philippines. At Angat Buhay, we strive to provide every Filipino child with accessible and inclusive quality education. Our organization focuses on literacy and numeracy interventions to promote a love for learning and ensure that students are well-prepared for employment. In collaboration with partners, we aim to provide facilities that enable effective learning delivery, particularly in the country's most vulnerable areas.

> INVOLVEMENTS

Angat Buhay has made it their mission to combat poverty in the Philippines. By collaborating with private companies and other non-governmental organizations, they can finance their initiatives and help address the needs of those living in impoverished communities referred to as "laylayan". The Angat Buhay Foundation has helped 20,131 families and 15,636 individuals across 176 communities in its first year. It is undeniable that the foundation has been able to make a significant impact on the lives of many people throughout the Philippines.
